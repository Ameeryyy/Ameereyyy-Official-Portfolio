My Journey

From humble beginnings in Alappuzha to building a global digital ecosystem. This is my path.

Joined Jyothinikethan Senior Secondary School. The start of formal education and the discovery of curiosity.
SSLC Results declared. Secured A+. A moment of huge relief and pride for the family, marking the end of childhood schooling.
Moved away from the traditional Humanities path. Found true passion in Game Development, Coding, and Technology.
Currently mastering Game Dev at Avoda Institute. Commuting daily, building this portfolio, and laying the foundation for Ameereyyy OS.
365+ Apps Goal. Launch of Global Infinity ID. Becoming a Global Tech Leader. The dream begins here.
